Why do I need a Google Business Profile for my business?

A verified GBP increases your visibility on Google Search and Maps, helps customers find you, shows your hours, services, photos, and allows you to collect reviews—all of which influence purchasing decisions.

What if my business is already on Google?

We can claim your listing, verify it, and ensure all information (hours, contact info, services, photos) is accurate and optimized to drive traffic.

How important are reviews for my business?

Extremely important—89% of consumers read online reviews before visiting a business, and even a single one-star improvement can boost revenue by up to 5–9%.
